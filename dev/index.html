CompositeBuildings.relate_buildings — Function

constructs a dataframe which contains two id columns, describing the relation between two dataframes. Both dataframes are expected to have at least an :id and an :geometry column. Two geometries g1, g2 are related, if: area(intersection(g1, g2)) >= min(area(g1), area(g2)) * overlap
